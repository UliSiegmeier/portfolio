BloodLink

social startup human centered design research UI design sketch invision proto.io

Since May 2017, I am part of the social startup “BloodLink” to improve blood availability worldwide, starting in Mumbai India. Working both in CIID (Copenhagen Institute of Interaction Design) and Mumbai, my main responsibilities are user and stakeholder interviews, UI design, and working with developers to implement the first software prototypes.
